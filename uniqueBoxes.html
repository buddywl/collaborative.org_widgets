Student Clubs and Organizations

Clubs provide low-stakes, supportive ways to engage socially and build confidence whether through creativity, community, advocacy, or leadership. Getting involved can bring structure, positive peer interactions, and a sense of belonging.

Bristol offers over 20 student-run clubs spanning cultural, professional, and interest-based groups:

  • Multicultural support groups (e.g., Black Student Union, LatinX Association)
  • Interest-based clubs: Gaming, Art, Culinary, Law, and more
  • Leadership roles in Student Senate and campus publications
